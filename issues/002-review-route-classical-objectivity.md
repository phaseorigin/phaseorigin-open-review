## Purpose
This issue collects critical comments on one bounded CHC claim family.
It is not a request to accept the full CHC program.

## Claim family
Durable shared facts / classical objectivity via record certificates.

## Standard tools retained
Open-system dynamics, decoherence, pointer-state analysis, record-formation models, observer consensus, and fragment-access models remain in scope.

## What CHC adds
A proposed certificate ladder linking durable local records to shared-fact status under explicit constraints, separating decohered states from publicly recoverable consensus-bearing records.

## Failure condition
The route fails if shared-fact status cannot be made explicit, if the proposed certificate conditions are weaker than established objectivity criteria, or if they collapse into ordinary decoherence/consensus language without exclusion value.

## Useful comments
- Missing standard references in quantum Darwinism, spectrum broadcast structure, pointer-state, or open-system literature
- Cases where the proposed certificate is weaker than established objectivity criteria
- Objections that the certificates are vacuous or overstate what the physics can certify
- Better formulations
- Reproducibility or formalization issues

## Not useful here
- General agreement/disagreement with the full framework
- Promotional comments
- Unbounded speculation
