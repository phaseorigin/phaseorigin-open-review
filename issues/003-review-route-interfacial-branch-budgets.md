## Purpose
This issue collects critical comments on one bounded CHC claim family.
It is not a request to accept the full CHC program.

## Claim family
Interfacial outcomes and branch budgets across sectors.

## Standard tools retained
Standard interactions, conservation intuition, response theory, material-state accounting, and statistical bookkeeping remain in scope.

## What CHC adds
Proposed typed accounting constraints for absorbed excitation, retained response, externalized output, and dissipative loss across interfaces, without claiming a full fit.

## Failure condition
The route fails if the constraints are vacuous, inconsistent, already captured by ordinary conservation bookkeeping, or trivially satisfied without adding predictive stress.

## Useful comments
- Missing standard references
- Cases where branch budgets fail or become arbitrary
- Examples where the accounting is already handled more cleanly by standard models
- Incorrect assumptions or boundary conditions
- Overstated claim labels
- Better formulations
- Reproducibility or formalization issues

## Not useful here
- General agreement/disagreement with the full framework
- Promotional comments
- Unbounded speculation
